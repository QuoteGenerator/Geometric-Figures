Icosphere

The Story


In a distant galaxy, the legendary Icosphere—an
ancient, glowing artifact of immense power—floated
in the heart of a collapsing star. Only the bravest
warrior could unlock its universe-altering secrets.

The Form


An icosphere is a geometric shape resembling a smooth,
spherical object composed of interconnected
equilateral triangles, balancing symmetry and
curvature, blending the structure of an icosahedron with the fluidity of a sphere.
